How to make a Tea

  1. Boil half cup of water
  2. Add sugar to taste
  3. Add one teaspoon tea leaves
  4. Add grinded ginger and boil
  5. After it buubles up, Add half cup milk and let it boil
  6. Tea is ready to serve!
